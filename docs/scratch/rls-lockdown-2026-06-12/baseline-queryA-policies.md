## Key confirmations vs AUDIT.md (from this partial dump)

- §5.1 CONFIRMED: legacy `Allow all on holiday_requests` / `Allow all on holiday_audit_log`
  FOR ALL USING(true) survive, plus ~17 `*_all` FOR ALL USING(true) policies
  (agencies, agency_connections, agency_operatives, agency_users, audit_logs,
  cis_records, contra_charges, daywork_sheets, document_audit_log, document_hub,
  document_packs, document_signoffs, hs_observations, job_documents,
  job_operatives, job_variations, labour_bookings, labour_proposals,
  labour_requests).
- WORSE THAN DOCUMENTED: `companies_delete_open` (DELETE USING(true)) and
  `companies_update_any` (UPDATE USING(true)) — anon can update/DELETE any
  company row. Removed by deploy4.
- §5.3 CONFIRMED: chat_messages delete/update/insert all `true`;
  aftercare_defects insert `true`; equipment_checks/equipment_defects insert `true`;
  inspections insert/update `true`; design_drawings + drawing_layers full CRUD `true`.
- Three policy generations coexist on documents/drawings/managers:
  `Company isolation *` (get_user_company_id), new-style `documents_*`
  (get_my_company_id + anon OR), and `Public read for documents` (true).
